ABOUT US

The Queen’s Blockchain Innovation Community (QBIC) is set out to bring together research committees and workshops within the Queen’s community and beyond. QBIC is the first student run body at Queen’s with a focus on fostering an interest and interdisciplinary understanding of blockchain technology. It brings together passionate students and faculty members from across all disciplines within the University to learn about adoption, computer science, legal, social and financial aspects of blockchain. It's mission is to turn Queen’s into a hub for blockchain talent and make Queen’s a place fostering interdisciplinary engagement and expertise around blockchain based innovations.

RESEARCH SCOPE

QBIC’s mission is to make Queen’s University the leading student-led academic research hub in Canada for the most disrupting technology the world has seen. In order for this to happen, it has never been more imminent for rigorous inter-faculty collaboration to take place - both for students and professors. Workshops on Cryptocurrency Adoption

This workshop aims to enable students to trade on cryptocurrency exchanges and learn how to store their cryptocurrency safely. The main barrier to investing in cryptocurrency is intimidation - i.e. it’s too complicated - and safety (what if I get hacked?). This workshop aims to make people more comfortable with investing in the crypto space with hopes that this knowledge will spread peer to peer.

Workshops on Blockchain Development

The QBIC Blockchain Development Series will teach software developers of all skill levels the concepts required to become successful at DApp and blockchain development. In these workshops, basic programming for blockchain applications is taught and attendees get to implement and test their blockchain ideas.

Presentations and Meetups

QBIC’s presentation and meetup series fosters a dialogue between students, faculty members and Kingston entrepreneurs, on topics chosen by students on QBICs research committee. With the motto: meet, learn, share - this series seeks to include guest speakers as well, whereby industry leaders can have a chance at engaging members of QBIC to learn about their experiences.
